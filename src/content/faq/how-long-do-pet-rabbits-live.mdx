## general lifespan

pet rabbits in Singapore typically live 7 to 12 years. some live longer with excellent care; some live shorter due to genetic, environmental, or care issues.

by breed (approximate):

- **Netherland Dwarf, Polish**: 8 to 11 years
- **Holland Lop, Lionhead, Mini Rex**: 7 to 14 years
- **Dutch**: 8 to 12 years
- **Mini Lop**: 7 to 10 years
- **French Lop, English Lop**: 5 to 8 years
- **Flemish Giant**: 5 to 8 years

larger breeds live shorter lives across all environments. lop-eared breeds and dense-coated breeds can have shorter SG lifespans if heat management is partial.

## what extends lifespan

three factors influence rabbit longevity meaningfully.

**spay or neuter**. unspayed female rabbits have a 60 to 80% lifetime risk of uterine cancer. spaying eliminates this risk and is the single largest lifespan-extending intervention for females. see our [neutering guide](/care/rabbit-neutering-singapore/).

**diet**. an 80% hay diet with proper portion control prevents the obesity, dental, and GI issues that shorten life. see [feeding rabbits in Singapore's climate](/care/feeding-rabbits-singapore-climate/).

**climate management**. consistent AC during hot months and prevention of heat stroke events directly impacts lifespan in SG.

other factors: regular vet care, mental enrichment, social bonding (a partner where appropriate), and absence of chronic stress.

## what shortens lifespan

- chronic dental issues, untreated
- chronic respiratory infections
- one or more heat stroke events
- recurring GI stasis
- obesity
- poor diet (low hay, high treats)
- chronic stress (noisy environment, frequent moves, lack of routine)

## the senior rabbit

rabbits over 5 years are considered senior. they need:

- more frequent vet checks (twice yearly)
- comfortable bedding for older joints
- dietary adjustments (sometimes more pellets to maintain weight)
- patience during slower movement and less play
- close attention to weight and droppings

a 10-year-old rabbit is broadly equivalent to a 70-year-old human. they deserve calm, comfortable conditions.

## the SG factor

a well-managed pet rabbit in SG can live as long as one in a temperate climate. the variable is climate management; partial AC, hot HDB flats, and unmanaged humidity events shorten lives. owners who invest in cooling and consistent care see SG rabbits at the long end of their breed range.
