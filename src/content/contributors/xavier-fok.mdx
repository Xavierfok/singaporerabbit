Xavier started keeping rabbits after adopting a rescue pair in 2018 and quickly
discovered most online rabbit-care content assumes a temperate climate, big
yards, and abundant farmers' markets. None of that maps to HDB life, year-round
30°C humidity, or a vet system where exotic-pet practice is a niche specialty.

This site is the answer to that gap, written from the perspective of an SG
owner doing the daily work. Vet directory entries are personally checked.
Care guides are reviewed against published rabbit-medicine sources and a
panel of SG owners and exotic vets before going live. Mistakes are corrected
in public — see the changelog on each guide.
