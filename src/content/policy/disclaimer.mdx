## not a vet

singapore rabbits is **not a veterinary practice**. nothing on this site is veterinary advice or
a substitute for it. we summarise community knowledge, link to peer-reviewed sources where we can,
and link to a real SG exotic-pet vet on every health-related post.

if your rabbit is sick, in pain, refusing food for >12 hours, or showing any sudden behavioural
change, **see a SG exotic-pet vet immediately**. start with our [vet directory](/vets/).

## not legal advice

posts about HDB pet rules, PDPA, or import-export of pets are summaries based on publicly
available SG government information. they're our best understanding at the time of writing
([see last_updated](/editorial-policy/)) and are not legal advice. confirm with the relevant
authority (HDB, AVS, ICA) before acting.

## directory accuracy

every directory entry is verified by us at the date shown in `last_verified`. clinics close.
hours change. prices move. we do not guarantee currency between verifications. if you spot stale
info, [tell us](/contact/) and we'll update.

## photos

all photos on this site are either (a) ours, (b) named contributors' with documented permission,
or (c) properly licensed stock. we never use FB-sourced photos. if you believe a photo is yours
and we've used it without permission, [contact us](/contact/) and we'll remove it within 24 hours.
