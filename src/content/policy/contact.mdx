we read every email. response time is usually under a week.

## by reason

- **directory submission** (you run a SG vet/groomer/shop/rescue/boarding and want to be listed):
  send name, address, hours, phone, website, and one sentence on rabbit experience. base listings
  are free.
- **correction**: spotted out-of-date hours or a closed clinic? tell us with a source link and
  we'll update within the week.
- **contributor pitch** (you're a SG rabbit owner and want a guest column or quote credit): send
  your handle, one paragraph on your rabbits, and a topic idea. we pay in credit + a link back to
  whatever you'd like (your social, your shop, etc.).
- **press / partnership**: keep it short, no decks please.

## what we don't take

- guest posts from PR firms or content marketing agencies
- AI-generated drafts (we'll know)
- "exposure" pitches from brands without SG presence
