## sourcing

we use four sources, in order of weight:

1. **named contributor input** — quotes, photos, and columns from real SG rabbit owners with
   permission documented in the repo
2. **public peer-reviewed material** — Rabbit Welfare Association & Fund, ASPCA, House Rabbit
   Society, plus SG-specific sources (NParks, AVS, exotic vet websites) where the topic is local
3. **community-discussion themes** — recurring questions and answers in public SG owner spaces.
   we capture themes, never republish posts. we never name members.
4. **our own owner experience** — clearly attributed when used

we do not pull from FB groups via automation. we never republish member posts.

## medical content

every health, illness, or symptom post:
- starts with "this is community-sourced information, not veterinary advice"
- includes "see a licensed SG exotic vet" in the first 100 words
- ends with a link to our [vet directory](/vets/)

we never name diagnoses, dosages, or treatments. we describe what owners commonly do, point to
peer-reviewed material, and route readers to a real vet.

## attribution

named contributors get:
- a byline on the post
- a photo credit on any photos they supply
- a link to whatever they choose (social, shop, blog)
- and a permanent listing on the [contributors page](/contributors/)

community-discussion themes are attributed as "owners commonly ask" or "a recurring question in
SG owner spaces is" — never to a specific member.

## fact-checking

every post has `published` and `last_updated` dates. we re-check posts:
- when GSC or Plausible flags a ranking drop or a click-through anomaly
- when a contributor or reader sends a correction
- on a 180-day rolling cadence regardless

corrections are made in-place with a one-line `## update {date}` note when material.

## affiliate programmes

we participate in:
- Shopee Affiliate (SG)
- Lazada Affiliate (SG)
- iHerb Affiliate (international)

every page with affiliate links includes a footer disclosure. clicking an affiliate link never
changes the recommendation — we recommend products we'd choose ourselves. if a brand pays us, it
goes on the [featured tier](/contact/) and is clearly marked as a paid placement.

## ai content

we use Claude and Codex for drafting, copy editing, and structuring posts. **every post is
reviewed by a human** before publishing. AI-generated drafts pass through the humanizer step
before publication. AI is a tool, not the author.

we never publish unedited AI output. we never republish AI-generated photos as if they were owner
photos.

## directory listings

base listings are free, forever. featured listings ($30/month) are clearly marked. featured tier
is capped at 5 listings per category. featured status does not change a base listing's content,
only its placement.

if a clinic closes, raises prices materially, or develops a pattern of bad reviews, we either
update the entry or remove it. reasoning is logged in the entry's `notes` field.

## privacy

our newsletter uses Buttondown (PDPA-compliant, double opt-in, unsubscribe in every email). we
do not sell your email. analytics is GA4 + Plausible, with IP anonymisation enabled.
